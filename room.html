Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our home services

How quickly can I get service?

For emergency services (plumbing, electrical), we typically respond within 1-2 hours. For standard services, we offer same-day or next-day appointments depending on availability. You can schedule services up to 90 days in advance through our online booking system.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes, all our service professionals are fully licensed, insured, and background-checked. We verify credentials before hiring and maintain ongoing training. Our insurance covers up to $2 million in liability protection for your peace of mind.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, upfront pricing. Most services have a diagnostic/service call fee (typically $79-$99) which is waived if you proceed with the repair. Labor rates vary by service type, and we provide detailed quotes before any work begins. There are no hidden fees or surprises.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we offer a 1-year warranty on all labor and a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our work, we'll return to fix it at no additional cost. Manufacturer warranties apply to parts and equipment we install.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs within a 50-mile radius. Enter your ZIP code in the quote form to check availability. We're expanding to new areas regularly, so check back if we're not currently in your location.
